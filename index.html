My Favourite Character,

Robert Downey Jr.

Robert Downey Jr. is my favourite movie character. He plays the role of Iron Man which I really love. Robert John Downey Jr.(born April 4, 1965) is an American actor and producer. In 2008, Downey was named by Time magazine among the 100 most influential people in the world. In 1992, Downey portrayed the title character in the biopic Chaplin, for which he was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Actor and won a BAFTA Award. He joined the TV series Ally McBeal, for which he won a Golden Globe Award.
